Welcome to Pilates in Parkwater

This boutique studio is well-appointed with traditionally crafted equipment. We use the reformer, tower unit, wunda chair along with additional smaller pieces of equipment to complement fundamental Pilates matwork.

Clients work privately with an instructor or may join a duo class where each student experiences a tailor made studio session. Having been introduced to the matwork as it was taught by Joseph Pilates, clients are then guided to use the various equipment pieces to aid and challenge their progress as desired.

Your instructor will work with you towards personal goals or outcomes, whether they be strength, flexibility, or rehabilitation.
